About Calvary Baptist Church

Pastor Joe Roof and Family

Rev. Joe Roof, B.A., M.A., has served as Senior Pastor of CBC for over twenty years. His wife, Kim, a native of the Capital Region, and their four precious children; Daniel, Naomi, Kyla, and Jaeden support him at his side as they labor together in the work of our Lord and Savior here at Calvary Baptist Church.

Pastor Paul Anobile

Pastor Paul Anobile is the Adult Bible Study teacher at Calvary Baptist Church. He was born in Brooklyn, New York. He graduated with his B.A. from the College of New Rochelle and his M.Div. from Mid-America Baptist Theological Seminary, becoming ordained in 1994. He invites both believers and skeptics to join him every Sunday morning and Wednesday evening here at our church as he offers systematic studies through various books of the Bible.
