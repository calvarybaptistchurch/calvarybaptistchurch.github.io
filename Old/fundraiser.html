Join Us to Support the Philippines

To help those affected by the recent devastating typhoon in the Philippines, join us for a fundraiser dinner on Saturday, December 14, along with a men's basketball game. Chef Geo, from our local area, will cook a Filipino Christmas meal for all to enjoy. All donations for the meal and the game will go to help the relatives of Albany Medical Center employees affected by the typhoon.

Boxes will also be available to donate to the Red Cross or to Kids International Ministries, an organization based in the Philippines that has committed to give every penny directly to the neediest people in regions hardest hit, such as Tacloban City and Eastern Samar.

We will hold this meal in our church building at 12 Launfal Street, Albany, NY. Suggested donations are $5 for the basketball game and $10 per adult/$5 per child for the meal. Register below to RSVP. For more information, you can call us at (518) 459-2717.

Order of Events

  • 1:00 PM: Benefit Men's Basketball Game
  • 3:00 PM: Filipino Buffet Meal
  • 4:00 PM: FYI time with speakers talking about the events and needs of the Philippines
  • 5:15 PM: 2nd FYI time
  • 6:00 PM: Concert by J. T. Sterle, an award-winning local 12-year-old pianist

If you are unable to attend, you can still give online via our direct donation page.
