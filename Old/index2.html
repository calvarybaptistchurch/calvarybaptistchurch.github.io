Pastor’s Update

Hello CBC Family and Friends,

Praise the Lord for one of the best Easter Sundays ever last week. There were record attendances for the breakfast and the service. The response to the program, “Wondrous Love,” was great!

My heart is filled with gratitude for all of God’s blessings. This Sunday, I will be sharing a message from the first five verse of Acts 16 on what it takes for churches to grow stronger and in numbers. We also have a baptism on Sunday.

Praise the Lord for His provision for new roofing here at CBC. Starr Roofing started their work on Monday and the gym and education buildings have been completed. As I write now, they are beginning the work on the sanctuary roof.

Spend time in God’s Word and in God’s church this weekend. I hope to see many of you on Sunday.

Have a great weekend.

Pastor Joe Roof
