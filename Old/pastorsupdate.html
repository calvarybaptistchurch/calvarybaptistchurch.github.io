Pastor’s Update

Hello CBC Family,

Here we are at the first day of March. Daylight Savings Time begins next Sunday (March 9) and Spring arrives on March 20. I have been sick all week and I appreciate Dennis Radford and others helping to fill in the gaps. I am still weak but looking forward to being there tomorrow to teach the Word of God. What does God want to accomplish in our hearts tomorrow? Pray, and let’s be there to see. We will be in Daniel 7 in the Adult Bible class and I will share a message entitled “Watch and Pray” in the morning service. We need your participation in the prayer service at noon. We will mention a special request regarding church finances at the prayer service.

Linda Newman is scheduled to come home from the hospital today. Kim Roof and Donna Heller were able to go and visit with her in my place and had a great time with Linda.

We will have an meeting about the Easter program after the noon service tomorrow.

Have a great weekend and I really look forward to seeing you again tomorrow. I greatly appreciate those who texted or called this week just to let me know you were praying. That was an encouragement to me. A couple of other pastors contacted me this week about sickness in their lives and families. Pray for them. Sometimes, these battles are not just physical, they are spiritual, and a prayerless assembly is a powerless assembly in such a battle.

Have a great weekend.

Pastor Joe Roof
